Sushi

Everyone can make sushi

Simple sushi recipe. If you can't make it you onlun9.

Ingredients

  • 1 fish of any kind
  • Some wasabi
  • 10ml Soysauce
  • 1 ginger

Steps

  1. Cut fish into thin slices.
  2. Grate wasabi.
  3. Cut ginger into thin slices.
  4. PUt everything on the plate and pour some soysauce on the side.
