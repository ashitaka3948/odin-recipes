Roast Chicken

Best roast chicken eva

This is da shit. Best roast chicken that you have ever fucking tasted. Make it right now.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 20oz butter

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with salt, peppper and garlic.
  2. Melt butter in saucepan.
  3. Pour butter on top of chicken.
  4. Bake in preheated oven at 220C for 1 hour 30 minutes.
